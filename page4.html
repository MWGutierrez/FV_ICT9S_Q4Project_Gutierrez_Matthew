ABOUT LAUFEY
  • Laufey (pronounced as lāy-vāy) is a singer-songwriter based in Los Angeles. She is an Icelandic singer known for her jazz songs with soft melodies and her calming voice. Her real name is Laufey Lín Bing Jónsdóttir. She also has a twin named Junia who is working as her creative director.

  • She wrote and recorded her second album, Bewitched, to carry on her story. Bewitched, which is a 21st-century twentysomething's point of view expressed via music, is influenced by jazz greats and classical masters. It also expands Laufey's aural pallet. The classic songcraft and intricate arrangements of tracks like the breezy bossa nova cut "From the Start" and the smoldering string-assisted ballad "Promise" instantly make them feel timeless, and Laufey's conversational lyrics make her music relatable to the next generation of jazz and classical enthusiasts.
